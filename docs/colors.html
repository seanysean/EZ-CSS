Quick info

Number of colors available: 14.

Here is a quick reference of all of the colors that Easy.CSS supports.

  1. white
  2. red
  3. dark-red
  4. light-green
  5. green
  6. dark-green
  7. light-blue
  8. blue
  9. orange
  10. yellow
  11. light-gray
  12. gray
  13. dark-gray
  14. black

A quick note: use gray, not grey when dealing with "gray" colors.

A quick example on using blue as a background-color. 
  <div class="ec-blue">
      <p>
        Text :)
      </p>
  </div>
  <!--The background of the <div> would be blue :)-->
